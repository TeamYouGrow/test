About Us

We started with a research question, which was 'Can we assist residents of urban areas to optimize gardening for users in a way that it saves precious resources'.

From this, we created a problem statement, which was to design a watering system that would automatically help houselholds save resources and take care of their plants using technology.

Our main objective is to optimize the user's experience growing plants using our technology.
